## Business Insights

* Electronics is the highest revenue-generating category.
* Laptop and Mobile are the best-performing products.
* Patna contributes the highest revenue.
* Adult customers generate the highest revenue.
* Revenue is primarily driven by Unit Price and Quantity.

## Conclusion

The business is heavily dependent on Electronics sales. Product pricing and purchase quantity are the strongest drivers of revenue, while customer age has little effect on sales performance.
