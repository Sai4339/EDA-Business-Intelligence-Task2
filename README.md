# Exploratory Data Analysis & Business Intelligence - Task 2

## Project Objective

The objective of this task is to uncover patterns, trends, and relationships within a retail sales dataset using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), SQL-based business querying, and Business Intelligence techniques.

## Dataset Overview

* Total Records: 1000
* Original Features: 12
* Engineered Features: 4
* Domain: Retail Sales

## Activities Performed

### Descriptive Statistics & Univariate Analysis

* Analyzed numerical and categorical variables.
* Generated histograms and bar charts.
* Identified key distributions and trends.

### SQL Business Analysis

* Answered business questions using SQL.
* Performed aggregation, filtering, and grouping operations.
* Extracted actionable business insights.

### Multivariate Analysis

* Investigated relationships among variables.
* Created scatter plots and correlation heatmaps.
* Evaluated factors influencing revenue.

### Key Insights

* Electronics is the highest revenue-generating category.
* Laptop and Mobile are the top-performing products.
* Patna generates the highest revenue among all cities.
* Adult customers contribute the highest revenue.
* Quantity and Unit Price strongly influence Total Sales.
